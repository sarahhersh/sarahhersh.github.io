About Me

Hello

some words about ME

Welcome! I am a journalism major at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at Maryland. I specialize in news reporting and I am proficient in photography and broadcasting.

01/ History

In high school I worked at my school new paper and that just further lit my passion for writing. Journalism has been a means for me for meet new people and further explore new passions and ideas!

02/ Mission

Going forward, I want to share my work on larger platforms and continue on my journey of exploring the world beyond myself.

03/ Vision

I look to work with the public and media to advocate for a brand or company. Learn how to apply real-world journalism skills to new digital media outlets. I have a willingness to discover what is going on in the world beyond people's doors and tells them about it.
